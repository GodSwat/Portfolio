Summary

From young age worked as an upholsterer and went to a highschool with a focus on business administration and accounting.

After highschool, I went to the UNLP aiming to become a public accountant but due to some issues and needs I've had to travel and move to a different city, and got into marketing instead, sadly as I've been needing to travel often for a few years I couldn't find enough time to focus and be dedicated with my studies, let alone being able to assist to all my classes or exams.
